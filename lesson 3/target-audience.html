Target Audiance Description

I want to be reaching out to anyone who is interested in the weather in their area. That would be ages 8 to 99.I want this to be accessible on across all devies, but primarily phone and tablet.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

There are a few hikes in the area that require good weather

Scenario 2

Traffic in the morning, especially around Farmington and Layton can be heavy. It would be good to know before hand if weather condidtions are making it worse

Scenario 3

If we plan a day at the park or go on a picnic it is best to do so in warm and calm weather

Scenario 4

Knowing the weather helps in knowing how to dress for the day.

Scenario 5

Wanting to know how to plan when to do outside chores
