Available tours

If you wanted to enjoy just a simple ride about Cozumel, this eco park is for you. This place offers views from atop of the lighthouse giving you a chance to see some very beautiful scenery of the beach and ocean.

Palancar Beach is a beautiful place to explore. Here you can do more than relax on a beach. Other than riding around the area in your amazing vehicle rented from Scoots 'N Jeeps, you can also go snorkeling from five dive locations and view some very beautiful and diverse marine life.

This is one of the few places where the sting-rays are trained to swim with humans. You can even feed and hold them if you want! Aside from the sting-rays you can also attend a seaside chocolate workshop.

If it's history and mystery you want, come and see some Mayan ruins. San Gervasio dates back to 600 AD in some areas, also known as the pre-Colombian Maya, and was considered a worship center for them.
